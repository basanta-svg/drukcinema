Our Mission

Making World-Class Cinema Accessible to All

DrukCinema was founded in 2024 with a single belief: every Bhutanese citizen deserves a world-class cinema experience — from the bustling capital of Thimphu to the serene valleys of Bumthang.

We combine Bhutan's rich cultural heritage with modern technology — integrating Bhutan NDI authentication, mobile-first booking, and locally-sourced film content — to build a platform that reflects the spirit of the Dragon Kingdom.

Instant e-ticket delivery with QR scan entry
Bhutan NDI integration for secure identity verification
Curated Bhutanese & international film catalogue
Real-time seat selection across all halls

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Accessibility

Cinema should be for everyone. We provide affordable tickets, easy online booking, and multiple payment options including Bhutan NDI.

Cultural Pride

We champion Bhutanese storytelling — from Dzongkha-language films to documentaries celebrating the kingdom's unique Gross National Happiness philosophy.

Sustainability

100% digital e-tickets reduce paper waste. Our infrastructure runs on renewable energy in partnership with Bhutan's green energy grid.

Security & Privacy

Integrated with Bhutan's National Digital Identity (NDI) system, we ensure the highest standards of data security and user privacy.

Excellence

From premium Recliner seats to crystal-clear Dolby sound systems, we partner only with cinemas that meet our quality standards.

Community

Cinema is a shared experience. We host community screenings, student programs, and partner with local film societies to grow Bhutan's film culture.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket on DrukCinema?
Browse movies on our homepage or the Movies page, click "Book Now" on any showing, select your preferred date, hall, showtime and seats, then confirm your booking. You'll receive an instant e-ticket with a QR code — no printing needed!
What is Bhutan NDI login and how does it work?
Bhutan NDI (National Digital Identity) is the government-issued digital identity system by DITT. You can use your NDI credentials to sign in or register on DrukCinema with biometric verification — no passwords needed. It's faster, safer, and fully verified.
Can I cancel or change my booking?
Yes. Go to My Profile → My Bookings and click "Cancel" on any confirmed booking. Free cancellation is available up to 2 hours before showtime. A 20% fee applies between 2–24 hours before. No refund within 2 hours of the show. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Bank of Bhutan cards, Bhutan Pay, TashiCell mobile payments, and other major debit/credit cards. NDI-linked payment is coming soon. Cash payment is available at the cinema counter.
How do I use my e-ticket at the cinema?
After booking, your e-ticket with QR code is shown on screen and emailed to you. At the cinema entrance, simply present the QR code on your phone for scanning. No printing required. The QR code is unique to your booking and can only be used once.
Do you offer student or group discounts?
Yes! Students get 25% off with a valid student ID using promo code STUDENT25. Group bookings of 10+ seats get a 15% discount — contact us at groups@drukcinema.bt for group arrangements. Check our Offers page for all current deals.
How do I get notified about new movies and releases?
Click the "Notify Me" button on any Coming Soon movie card (you need to be signed in). You'll also receive updates if you subscribe to our newsletter. Follow us on social media @DrukCinema for the latest announcements.
